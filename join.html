Why join MSBT?

Our next application period will start in the middle of January 2024

Any undergraduate student with an interest in synthetic biology with biological engineering is welcome to join! Absolutely no previous experience is required.

Our team is composed of undergraduate students from a variety of majors, including (but not limited to):

  • Molecular, Cellular, and Developmental Biology
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Biology
  • Mathematics
  • Biochemistry
  • Biophysics
  • Neuroscience
  • Microbiology
  • Philosophy
  • Materials Science & Engineering
  • Computer Science
